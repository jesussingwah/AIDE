What does AIDE aim to achieve?

AIDE is the newest invention on a way to get your household chores done and still have time for your family. Our app/website will have tabs specifying what services are available to request. Once you open up the website/app it'll be like a social media type platform where you see the latest post however it'll be the most recent request. The Agent can then accept the request and assist you with what you want or need. Our goal is to help those that might not have the time to do there daily chores.
